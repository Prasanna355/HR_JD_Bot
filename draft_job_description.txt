**Job Description:**

We are seeking a skilled and experienced Content Manager to join our team at KoworkerAI. The successful candidate will be responsible for creating and managing engaging content across various platforms to increase brand awareness and conversions.

**Key Responsibilities:**

1. Develop and implement a content strategy that aligns with the company's business goals.
2. Create and publish engaging content, including blog posts, articles, social media posts, and email campaigns.
3. Manage the company's content calendar, ensuring timely and high-quality content delivery.
4. Collaborate with designers, developers, and other stakeholders to produce compelling content.
5. Edit, proofread, and improve content written by other team members.
6. Conduct keyword research to optimize content for search engines and increase organic traffic.
7. Analyze content performance and adjust strategies as needed.
8. **Stay updated with the latest content trends and technologies, and apply best practices to the company's content strategy.**

**Requirements:**

1. Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Journalism, or a related field.
2. Proven experience as a Content Manager, Copywriter, or similar role.
3. Excellent writing and editing skills in English.
4. Strong knowledge of content management systems, social media platforms, and SEO principles.
5. Familiarity with analytics tools, such as Google Analytics.
6. Proficiency in MS Office, Adobe Creative Suite, and email marketing software.
7. Strong project management and organizational skills.
8. **Experience with content creation and management tools, such as Canva, Hootsuite, or similar.**
